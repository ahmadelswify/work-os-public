# Career Portfolio Building

Turn your daily work into a compelling career story with metrics, impact, and evidence.

## The Problem

When it's time for:
- Performance reviews
- Promotion discussions
- Job interviews
- LinkedIn updates

You can't remember what you accomplished, forget the metrics, and undersell your impact.

## The Solution

AI automatically captures your wins and builds your portfolio as you work.

*A data-driven approach to reducing churn through proactive customer management*

## Overview
Built an automated customer health scoring system that reduced churn by 15% and enabled proactive account management for 200+ customers.

## The Problem
- Customer Success team was reactive, only engaging when customers complained
- No visibility into which customers were at risk
- Churn was 22% annually (industry avg: 18%)
- Team spent time on wrong accounts while high-value customers churned

## The Solution
Created SQL-based dashboard in BI tool (Tableau/Looker/Sigma) that:
- Aggregates 12 health signals (product usage, support tickets, NPS, engagement)
- Calculates risk scores updated daily
- Prioritizes accounts needing attention
- Tracks trends over time

## My Role
- **Research:** Interviewed 10 CSMs and analyzed churned customers to identify signals

## Tips

1. **Log wins immediately**: Don't wait for review time
2. **Capture metrics**: Numbers make stories credible
3. **Note recognition**: Save praise from stakeholders
4. **Use STAR format**: Situation, Task, Action, Result
5. **Update LinkedIn**: Share wins publicly (appropriately)
6. **Tell stories**: Context + action + impact is compelling

## Getting Started

1. Create `Career/accomplishments.md` and `Career/portfolio/` directory
2. Start logging wins: "Add to accomplishments: [what you did]"
3. Ask AI to turn major projects into portfolio pieces
4. Review quarterly: "Show me my accomplishments this quarter"
5. Use for reviews, interviews, LinkedIn updates

---

**Pro tip**: The best time to document your impact is right after you achieve it. Future you will thank present you!
